How to play

Find the 6 secret words in 6 tries.

  • Each clue gives you a word and a number; choose that many words related to the clue (e.g., HAT 2 means pick 2 words related to a hat).
  • Selecting a non-related word ends the round.

Example: HAT 2

  • HEAD(Guessed & Intended)
  • FACE(Intended clue)
  • JUPITER(Incorrect)

Round ended early after guessing JUPITER.

Example: LODGE 3

  • POOL(Guessed & Intended)
  • KING(Guessed & Intended)
  • EAGLE(Intended clue)
  • GERMANY(Guessed by chance)

Round ended after 3 guesses of the secret words.

The AI considers words similar if they are often used in the same context: MODERATE can be a clue for DEGREE, or BOOM for SHACK. It will also learn from user behavior.

A new puzzle is released daily at midnight, but you can play unlimited puzzles from earlier days.
